Chief Complaint: Stomach ache occurring mostly during the night.

History of Present Illness:

Past Medical History:
- Mild gastritis diagnosed 2 years ago.
- No known ulcers or GI bleeding.
- No chronic medication use.

Family History:
- Father had peptic ulcer disease.
- Mother has type 2 diabetes.

Lifestyle:
- Frequently skips dinner due to late work hours.
- Drinks 2–3 cups of coffee per day.
- Occasional alcohol on weekends.
- No smoking or drug use.

Assessment/Plan:
- Possible differential diagnoses include peptic ulcer disease (especially duodenal ulcer), functional dyspepsia, or mild acid reflux.
- Recommended upper GI endoscopy for further evaluation.
- Start trial of proton pump inhibitor (PPI) for 2 weeks.
- Advise regular meals and reduction of caffeine intake.
